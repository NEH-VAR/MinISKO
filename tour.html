ACADEMICS

ADMIN Building

ADMIN Wing

The Admin Wing, located on the left side of the Administration Building, accommodates the majority of faculty and technical staff. This includes essential offices such as the Office of Student Affairs (OSA), the Registrar's Office, and the Director's Office, among others.

CHSS Wing

The CHSS Wing, situated on the right side of the Administration Building, houses important administrative offices, including the Dean's office, the Registrar's office, and the Office of Anti-Sexual Harassment (OASH). This wing also features classrooms for various programs, such as Bachelor of Arts in Communication and Media Arts (BACMA), Bachelor of Arts in Education (BAE), and Architecture (ARCHI), supporting a diverse range of academic disciplines.

College of Science and Mathematics

The CSM Building, located at a distance from the main campus, primarily houses classrooms for programs in Computer Science, Biology, Applied Mathematics, and Food Technology. It is equipped with a variety of laboratories dedicated to computer science, agriculture, biology, and agriculture, providing students with hands-on learning experiences in their respective fields.

Library

The library, situated adjacent to the Administration and EBL dormitory, offers a diverse collection of books and access to Macintosh computers. Additionally, patrons can enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi services throughout the facility.

Oblation Plaza

The Oblation Plaza features the Oblation statue or 'Oble', a symbol of selfless dedication to the nation. This remarkable work is crafted by National Artist Guillermo Tolentino and serves as a poignant representation of the spirit of service and sacrifice for the country.

Kalimudan Covered Court

The covered court, located adjacent to the Kalimudan canteen, serves as a versatile space for recreational activities. This facility is often used for various sports, events, and gatherings, providing students and faculty with an area to engage in physical activities and social interactions in a sheltered environment.

Dormitories

Elias B. Lopez Hall

The Elias B. Lopez Hall, commonly known as the EBL Dormitory, is a housing facility designed for UP students seeking affordable accommodation near campus. While it offers cost-effective living arrangements, residents are expected to adhere to strict rules and regulations to maintain a conducive living environment. The dormitory aims to foster a sense of community among students while providing essential amenities for their academic and personal needs.

CSM Annex

The CSM Dormitory is specifically designed to accommodate students who are in the process of completing their thesis or conducting research. This dormitory provides a supportive environment for graduate students, offering essential amenities and resources that facilitate their academic work. With a focus on creating a conducive atmosphere for study and collaboration, the CSM Dormitory helps students manage their time effectively as they work towards their academic goals.

Sport Facilities

Sports Complex

Training Facility

The Training Facility is an indoor gym designed to enhance the performance and skills of athletes. It is equipped with a wide range of state-of-the-art equipment, including weightlifting machines, cardiovascular machines, resistance training tools, and functional training gear. This facility aims to provide athletes with the resources they need to improve their strength, endurance, agility, and overall athletic performance. Additionally, the Training Facility may offer specialized training programs, coaching, and support to help athletes reach their full potential.

Open Field

An open field used for archery and potentially other sports can be referred to as an "archery range" or "archery field." If the space is versatile and accommodates multiple sports, it may be called a "multi-sport field" or "sports field."

Stadium

The newly built stadium in Davao, designed to host national events and complement the existing JMC Stadium near Davao Airport, can be referred to as a "state-of-the-art sports stadium" or "multi-purpose stadium." This designation emphasizes its modern facilities and capability to accommodate a variety of sporting events, concerts, and large gatherings.
